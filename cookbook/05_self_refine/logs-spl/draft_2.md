## Pressure’s Faint Return

Waves diminish slow,
Subpulses, tiny waves flow,
Silence defines sound.

**Understanding SPL**

Sound Pressure Level, or SPL, measures sound intensity in decibels (dB). It’s not just loudness, but the pressure of sound waves – crucial for concert halls and hearing tests.

**Haiku’s Precision**

The haiku’s form – five, seven, and five syllables – forces a focused look at sound. It’s perfect for capturing the subtle nature of SPL, a measurement of pressure against silence.
