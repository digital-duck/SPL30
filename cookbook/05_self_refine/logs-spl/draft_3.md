## Silence’s Gentle Fall

Sound fades, soft and low,

**Understanding SPL**

Sound Pressure Level, or SPL, represents how much pressure a sound makes when it hits an object – measured in decibels (dB). This delicate balance reflects the precision of SPL, a measurement of sound intensity, crucial for capturing the subtle nuances that define concert halls and hearing tests.
