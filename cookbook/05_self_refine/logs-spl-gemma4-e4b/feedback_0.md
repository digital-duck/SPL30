## 🛠️ Areas for Refinement (Minor Edits for Perfection)

These suggestions are minor polishes rather than fixes, designed to elevate the piece from excellent to flawless.

### 1. Introduction (General)
*   **Suggestion:** In the opening of the Blog Post, consider slightly intensifying the emotional hook.
*   **Example Tweak:** Instead of starting with "In our hyper-connected, always-on world, stress has become the default setting," you could try a rhetorical question or a more vivid imagery hook: *"Do you ever feel like your brain is running 100 tabs at once? In our hyper-connected, always-on world, stress isn't an exception—it's the default setting."* (This makes the problem feel more immediate to the reader.)

### 2. Academic Overview (Technical Polish)
*   **Suggestion:** Ensure consistency in terminology. While "cognitive defusion" is correct, briefly defining it within the text (e.g., *“By developing cognitive defusion—the ability to observe a thought as just a thought, rather than a fact…”*) adds value for readers who might be unfamiliar with the highly technical language.

### 3. Formatting/Readability (General)
*   **Suggestion:** In all three options, using **bolding** for key takeaways (e.g., **Cortisol Killer**, **Prefrontal Cortex**, **3–5 minutes a day**) helps the reader skim and reinforces the most critical points. You do this well, but ensure this technique is used consistently across the final drafts.
